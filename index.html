Who Are We?

Pioneering the Future of Autonomous Swarms

We are a team of robotics researchers and engineers from Lancaster University, dedicated to bringing cutting-edge swarm intelligence technology from the lab to real-world applications. With over 40 years of collective experience in autonomous systems, we're transforming how industries approach complex challenges through coordinated multi-robot solutions that are smarter, safer, and more efficient than traditional methods.

The Problem

Data Assumptions

Traditional systems rely on pre-mapped environments and predictable conditions. Real-world scenarios are dynamic and uncertain.

Limited Mission Time

Single robot deployments face battery constraints and coverage limitations. Critical tasks demand extended operational capacity.

Risk of Failure

Individual robot failure means mission failure in traditional approaches. High-stakes environments cannot afford single points of failure.

No Adaptation

Conventional systems follow fixed protocols without learning. Complex challenges require intelligent response to changing conditions.

Our Applications

Nuclear Decommissioning

Advanced swarm robotics for safe nuclear facility inspection. Reducing human exposure in hazardous environments.

Environmental Monitoring

Comprehensive ecosystem tracking with distributed sensor networks. Real-time data collection across vast natural habitats.

Methane Quantification

Precise detection and measurement of methane emissions. Supporting climate change mitigation through advanced monitoring.

Search and Rescue

Rapid deployment swarms for emergency response operations. Coordinated search patterns covering large areas efficiently.
